Chicken Garlic Butter Recipe

Ingredients:
- 2 boneless chicken breasts
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 4 cloves garlic (minced)
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano (optional)
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Fresh parsley (for garnish)

Instructions:
1. Clean and pat dry the chicken breasts.
2. Season both sides with salt, pepper, paprika, and oregano.
3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
4. Add the chicken and cook for about 5-6 minutes on each side until golden brown and fully cooked.
5. Remove chicken and set aside.
6. In the same pan, melt butter and add minced garlic.
7. Cook garlic for 1-2 minutes until fragrant (do not burn).
8. Return the chicken to the pan and coat it with the garlic butter.
9. Cook for another 2 minutes, spooning the sauce over the chicken.
10. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.

Serving Suggestions:
- Serve with rice, pasta, or steamed vegetables.
- Add a squeeze of lemon juice for extra flavor.

Notes:
- Ensure chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C).
- You can substitute chicken thighs for a juicier option.
