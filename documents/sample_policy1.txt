Remote Work Policy

Employees are permitted to work remotely up to two days per week with manager approval.
All remote access to company systems must use VPN and multi-factor authentication.
Sensitive company data must not be stored on personal devices.
Employees must complete annual security awareness training.

Leave Policy

Full-time employees receive 15 days of paid time off annually.
Unused PTO may be carried over up to 5 days into the following calendar year.
Sick leave is tracked separately from paid time off.

Access Control Policy

System access is granted based on job responsibilities and least privilege principles.
Managers must submit access requests for new employees.
All privileged access must be reviewed quarterly.
